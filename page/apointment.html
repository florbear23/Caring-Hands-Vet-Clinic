    Step 5: Terms and Conditions for Online Appointment Booking

    Instructions for Clients Before Making an Appointment:

    New Clients

    • Arrival Time: Please arrive 15 minutes before your appointment and bring all your previous medical records.
    • Medical records: Please have your previous veterinarian/emergency hospital/breeder email us all the important vaccine history and medical records to the clinic's email 24 hours before your appointment - Email: info@caringhandsvetclinic.com
    • Appointment Restrictions: New clients cannot book technician appointments.
    • Routine Check-Ups and GI Issues: If your pet is coming in for a routine annual check-up or has gastrointestinal issues, please bring a fresh stool sample in a plastic bag or small container completely sealed.

    Existing Clients

    • Arrival Time: Please arrive 10 minutes before your appointment to be checked in.
    • Technician Appointments: When you select a technician appointment, your pet will be seen by a technician to update vaccines, perform blood tests, and deworming under the supervision of a doctor. If you need to discuss any health concerns with the doctor, please cancel and change the appointment type to a Doctor's Appointment.
    • If your pet is up-to-date on their annual or biannual check-up, services like booster vaccines, heartworm tests, other lab work, Cytopoint injections, and Allercept injections can be performed by an experienced or certified Caring Hands technician.
    • Doctors Appointment: If your pet is experiencing any health or behavior concerns and you need to see a doctor during your visit, please select the "Doctor Appointment" option.
    • Annual and Biannual check-up: Schedule a Doctor Appointment (not a technician exam) if your pet is due for an annual check-up and vaccines.
    • No-Show Policy: Please notify the office at least 24 business hours in advance if you cannot make it to your pet's appointment. Clients will lose their deposit if they do not show up for the appointment. Calling the office after the appointment time or within that hour will be considered a no-show.
    You must agree to the terms and conditions to proceed.
