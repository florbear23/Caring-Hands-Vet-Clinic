    Skin Infections

    Skin infections can cause itching or swelling and prevent your pet from being active. At Caring Hands Vet Clinic in Philadelphia, PA, we provide various treatments to relieve any discomfort your cat or dog is experiencing and prevent further complications. Before you visit us, learn about some signs of skin infections and why treatment is important below:

    Identifying Skin Infections

    Pets can develop skin infections due to a multitude of factors, including bacterial, fungal, or parasitic organisms. Common signs of a skin infection in pets include redness, itching, hair loss, scabs, and foul odor. If you notice any of these symptoms, it's essential to consult our veterinarian for guidance.

    The Role of Pet Dermatology

    Pet Dermatology is a specialized field dedicated to diagnosing and treating skin conditions in animals. We have the experience and resources to identify the root cause of your pet's skin infection accurately. By conducting thorough examinations and utilizing advanced diagnostic tools, we can develop customized treatment plans tailored to your furry friend's specific needs.

    Seeking Prompt Care

    It‚Äôs important to seek prompt treatment for skin infections to prevent the condition from worsening and causing your cat or dog further discomfort. Delaying care can lead to complications and may require more extensive interventions down the line. By seeking timely veterinary care, you can ensure that your pet receives the necessary care to alleviate its symptoms and help it heal.

    Pet Allergy Treatment

    Pet allergies can exacerbate skin conditions and contribute to the development of infections. Identifying and managing your pet's allergies is vital for his or her overall health and comfort. We can perform allergy testing to identify specific triggers and recommend appropriate treatments, such as allergen-specific immunotherapy or dietary modifications.
