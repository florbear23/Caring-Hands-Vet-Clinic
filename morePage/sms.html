    Privacy Policy

    At Caring Hands, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard customer data.

    Consumer Information Policy

    Consumer information is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes under any circumstances. Your trust is paramount, and we take stringent measures to maintain your privacy.

    Information Collection

    We collect customer information to provide better services and improve user experiences. The types of information we may collect include:

    • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address to communicate with you regarding your inquiries, orders, or account.
    • Transaction Details: Payment information and purchase history to process transactions securely.
    • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website or services to enhance functionality and user experience.

    How We Use Your Information

    • To process and fulfill orders or service requests.
    • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
    • To improve our website, products, and services based on user feedback.
    • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.

    Data Security

    We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

    Your Rights

    You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Please contact us directly for assistance with data-related requests.

    Questions or Concerns

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, feel free to contact us at [Contact Information].
